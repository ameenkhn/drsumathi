Our Story

Healing Thanjavur with Advanced Surgery Since 2008

Founded in 2008 as Sudar Hospital by Dr. S. Sasiraj in a rented building, the hospital moved to its present location as a modern facility — Sudar Hospital And Research Institute — inaugurated on 25 November 2013. It was developed and established by a reputed surgeon and son of the soil, Dr. S. Sasiraj (MS, DNB), at Thanjavur, Tamil Nadu.

Today it stands as one of the most distinguished multispecialty surgical centres, providing advanced surgical services. In addition to General & Laparoscopic Surgeries, the hospital offers dedicated dialysis and ophthalmology services, with stringent infection prevention and control standards.

  • Pioneer in laparoscopic surgery in the Thanjavur region
  • Multispecialty under one roof — surgery, ophthalmology & 24×7 dialysis
  • Stringent infection prevention and control standards for every patient

What Drives Us

Our Guiding Principles

The vision, mission and values that shape every decision we make for the patients who trust us with their care.

Vision

To become a Multi Specialty Surgical centre with state-of-the-art facilities and international standards, providing each healthcare seeker a sense of contentment and peace through compassionate care, commitment and responsibility in work.

Mission

To provide general, laparoscopic and day-care surgeries for patients in rural South India with the highest standards of safety and quality, minimal length of stay, and in a cost-effective manner.

Dr. S. Sasiraj MS, DNB

General & Laparoscopic Surgeon

Dr. S. Sasiraj did his MBBS at Thanjavur Medical College (1989) and his MS in General Surgery at B.J. Medical College, Pune (1996). Over a span of 17 years, he has successfully performed more than 30,000 general and laparoscopic surgeries.

He is the pioneer of laparoscopic surgery in Thanjavur, bringing minimally-invasive surgical care and quicker recovery to the region.

Dr. G. Rohini MBBS, DO, DNB

Ophthalmic Surgeon

Dr. G. Rohini is an ophthalmic surgeon with over 30 years of clinical experience in Thanjavur. She completed her MBBS at Thanjavur Medical College and her postgraduate training at Aravind Eye Hospital, Madurai.

She has performed more than 15,000 cataract surgeries and specialises in state-of-the-art phacoemulsification cataract surgery, comprehensive glaucoma management and diabetic retinopathy evaluation. She also leads a free eye check-up every Thursday for the underprivileged.
