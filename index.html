About Sudar Hospital

A Trusted Name in Surgical Care Since 2008

Founded in 2008 by a reputed surgeon and son of the soil, Dr. S. Sasiraj (MS, DNB), Sudar Hospital grew into Sudar Hospital And Research Institute in 2013. Today it stands as one of the most distinguished multispecialty surgical centres in Thanjavur, providing the most advanced surgical services.

Alongside general and laparoscopic surgery, we offer dedicated dialysis and ophthalmology services — delivering high-quality patient care while adhering to stringent infection prevention and control standards.

  • Advanced surgical infrastructure — modular OTs, ICU & CSSD sterilisation
  • Pioneer in laparoscopic surgery in the Thanjavur region
  • Affordable, day-care friendly with minimal length of stay

Specialities

Comprehensive Surgical & Medical Services

A full spectrum of surgical specialities supported by modern diagnostics, in-house pharmacy and round-the-clock critical care.

General & Laparoscopic Surgery

Laparoscopic cholecystectomy, hernioplasty, appendicectomy, thyroidectomy, haemorrhoidectomy and more.

Ophthalmic Surgery

Phacoemulsification cataract surgery, glaucoma care and diabetic retinopathy evaluation.

Nephrology & Dialysis

24×7 dialysis, AV fistula creation and central line insertion with strict infection control.

Orthopaedics

Trauma, fracture care and joint procedures supported by modern imaging and theatre facilities.

Urology

Diagnosis and surgical management of urinary tract and related conditions.

Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Women's health surgeries including hysterectomy (TAH, VH, LAVH) and related procedures.
